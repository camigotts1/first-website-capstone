Products We Love

This wine is so lovely. Light, slightly effervescent, lightly sweet. It pairs well with food or by itself (or mixed in a cocktail!). At only $5 after tax, it's really hard to beat. I always have a bottle of this to sip by myself, or to share. There is also a rose version that is almost the same in taste, but...er...rosier.
These sweet empanadas are so delicious. Basically like a pumpkin hand pie. I usually prepare these in the toaster oven, and they get a nice crispy crust, but sometimes when I am impatient I will just microwave them. The only complaint I have about these is that they are only seasonal!! And they sell out QUICK.
I resisted buying these for so long. How special could ice cream in a cone really be?? But these are. I don't know. Something about the cone is sooooo tasty. There's a bit of chocolate on the bottom to keep the ice cream from melting out the cone hole. My favorite are the vanilla, because I don't care for the chocolate cone. I wish they had a mint or some other flavor with the classic cone.
I just recently tried these. Obsessed. They have a bit of a kick but aren't as hot as hot Cheetos (if you're a weenie like me and think hot cheetos are spicy). So tasty. So bad for you.
I love this proscuitto. Specifically this one. The other kinds are ok, but this is *chef's kiss*. I eat this on the drive home from the store. I used to work in the charcuterie section of Whole Foods, and I still get this. I mean, you really can't beat the price ($4??). It's light, salty, tender.
I am a simple woman. I like ube. I like ice cream. I buy this every time it's in stock. It's a good amount of sweetness (could be less sweet, but it has to appeal to the masses y'know?). It's so creamy. I will say, it does have a slight *grittiness* to it, which doesn't bother me at all, but I do know some people may have texture issues.
